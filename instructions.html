Instructions

Step 1

Application: Open the back zipper and gently place your injured limb inside. The splint will automatically inflate and cool upon chemical activation, providing both support and swelling reduction.

Step 2

Activation: To activate the splint, apply pressure to the inner layer to mix the sodium bicarbonate and citric acid. This action breaks the compartment boundary and starts the chemical reaction.

Step 3

Adjustment: Ensure the splint fits snugly but not too tightly. The design minimizes the need for adjustments, making it simple to use even when you're alone.

General Injury Treatment Awareness for Athletes

As an athlete, being aware of basic injury treatment principles is crucial. Here's what you should know:

↣ Immediate Action: At the first sign of injury, stop your activity. Continuing to stress an injured area can worsen the injury.

↣ Understand Your Limits: Recognize the difference between pushing your limits and risking injury. Listening to your body is key! Regular Training and Warm-up.

↣ Prevention Methods: The combination of regular, balanced training, and proper warm-up routines can significantly reduce the risk of sports injuries.

↣ Seek Professional Help: For severe injuries, consult a healthcare professional immediately. An accurate diagnosis is crucial for effective treatment and recovery.

R.I.C.E. Method

Remember this acronym for initial injury treatment

↣ Rest: Avoid putting weight on the injured area.

↣ Ice: Apply ice to reduce swelling and pain. However, never apply ice directly to the skin; use a cloth barrier.

↣ Compression: Use bandages to apply gentle pressure and reduce swelling.

↣ Elevation: Keep the injured area raised above heart level to decrease swelling.

By integrating these practices into your routine and understanding how to use our inflatable cooling splint effectively, you can better protect yourself against injuries and manage them more effectively when they occur. Stay safe and stay informed!
