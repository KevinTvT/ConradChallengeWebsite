Step 1

Wrap the splint around the injured area.

Step 2

Zip the back of the splint up so that it forms a sort of sleeve around the injury.

Step 3

Uncork both layers

Step 4

Pour water in making sure not to go over the specified line.

Step 5

Plug the outer cork back in to keep the air from leaking out

Step 6

Wait for about 2-3 minutes for the reaction to finish taking place. The reaction has stopped when there are no more fizzing in the inner layer of the splint.

Step 7

Plug the inner cork back in to keep the air and liquid solution from mixing
